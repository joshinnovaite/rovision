# ST-10.4: Segment-Then-Measure §18 Scaffold (Sky / Water)

Parent: [[BOW-10-E1-Phase-0-Proof-Of-Capability]]
Status: done
Progress: done — new §18 written in [[subsea_defect_demo.ipynb]], gated on `DOMAIN=='e1'`. SAM 2 masks a
sky region and a water region from coarse box prompts, then a classical descriptor reads each: sky →
clear/overcast (blue-ratio + saturation), water → glassy/rippled/choppy (Laplacian high-frequency energy).
Frame-level scene attribute, not a tracked instance; does **not** feed YOLO. Thresholds are uncalibrated
first-guesses, to tune on real frames ([[DR-017-Segment-Then-Measure-Scene-Attributes]]).

## Preconditions
- E1 registered ([[ST-10.1-E1-Domain-Registration]]); §18 gates on `DOMAIN=='e1'`.
- SAM 2 promptability available for region masking ([[sam2-as-engine]]); no detector/class involved.

## Steps
1. Add §18 to the notebook, gated on `DOMAIN=='e1'`.
2. Prompt SAM 2 with coarse sky-region and water-region boxes; obtain two masks.
3. Sky descriptor: blue-ratio + saturation → clear/overcast.
4. Water descriptor: Laplacian variance / high-frequency energy → glassy/rippled/choppy.
5. Emit per-frame scene attributes (outside the detect→segment instance path).

# ST-10.9: §18b Calibration Sweep + Threshold Calibration

Parent: [[BOW-10-E1-Phase-0-Proof-Of-Capability]]
Status: done
Progress: done (recorded retroactively) — ran the §18b segment-then-measure sweep on real Como footage and
calibrated thresholds. **Findings refine [[DR-017-Segment-Then-Measure-Scene-Attributes]]:** fixed
rectangular prompt boxes are scene-contaminated (boats, wakes, hull, terrain, AR graphics leak into the
sky/water boxes); reliable signals are **sky brightness** (open sky readable only on the ONBOARD camera —
4 of 5 clips read "no open sky") and **water specular fraction** (cleanly separates calm vs wake-wash).
**Laplacian roughness too contaminated** for a 3-level chop scale → water shipped as an honest binary
(calm / wake-wash); 3-level chop **deferred** to ST-10.11.

## Preconditions
- §18 scaffold present ([[ST-10.4-Segment-Then-Measure-S18-Scaffold]]).
- Real E1 footage staged and run ([[ST-10.5-E1-Footage-Capture]]).

## Steps
1. Sweep §18b over the 5 clips; inspect masked sky/water regions and their descriptors.
2. Diagnose contamination (boats/wakes/terrain/AR graphics in fixed boxes); identify trustworthy signals.
3. Calibrate thresholds: sky clear if median brightness ≥ 0.45 AND blue_ratio ≥ 0.33 AND saturation ≥ 0.15,
   else overcast if bright, else no_sky; water wake-wash if median specular_frac ≥ 0.30 else calm.
4. Demote roughness/chop to a deferred item; ship water as binary calm/wake-wash.

## Outcome — confirmed insulators taxonomy
Final 4 classes: **insulator_string** (the disc-chain assembly — the hero), **cross_arm**, **conductor**,
**pylon_body**.

Deliberately excluded (anti-patterns from [[DR-014-Domain-Viability-Gated-By-Object-Scale]]):
- **`insulator_disc`** — the swarm trap: dozens of tiny near-identical discs per frame.
- **`foundation`** — the sparse trap: almost never visible in elevated inspection shots.

Framing is **component/inventory**, not defect: the footage shows healthy insulators (nothing to flag).
Defect mode would require defect footage.
